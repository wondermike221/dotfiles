Here are some troubleshooting steps you can take. (if none of this helps we can schedule a time to connect over a remote session):

- Restart your computer
- clear your cache & cookies: click on the three dots at the top right of the browser (or three lines) -> change the time period to longer if necessary -> click delete data
- Run the ITSS health check tool and copy the results here
- run a sync in company portal: click on the gear at the bottom left -> click sync
- check the compliance tab in software center (screenshot any errors if there are any)
- visit https://devicecheck.corp.ebay.com and note the results
